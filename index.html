About

We love Pizza and so will you after you indulge.

Afro-taliano was founded on a dream by young entreprenuers to bring nothing but the best pizzas to their community for them to enjoy pizzas with rich african flavor and made with authenticity.Afro-italiano was founded in 2017 and ever since has employed the youth and sourced their products from young kenyans supporting their hustle in turn.



Freshly sourced farm products


Our pizza is rated as the best pizza in Africa bringing Italian authenticity with the rich african flavour and farm sourceed ingredients
